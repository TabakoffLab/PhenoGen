You can correlate your phenotype data with the BXD recombinant inbred mouse brain data on the website

  1. Select the Public BXD RI Mice dataset on the Datasets View of the Analyze Microarray Data tab.
  2. Choose one of the normalized versions of the dataset and the correlation type of analysis.
  3. Enter or upload your phenotype data for the BXD RI strains, filter out some of the probes, and run the correlation analysis.
