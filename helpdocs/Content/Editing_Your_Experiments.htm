Editing Your Experiments

If you want to change the experiment design types or factors of an experiment, you must edit your experiment and download a new empty spreadsheet.

  1. Choose Microarray Analysis Tools in the main menu.
  2. Click Upload your own data. The Upload Arrays page displays and shows a list of Microarray Experiments you have entered.
  3. Click the pencil icon beside the experiment you want to edit.
