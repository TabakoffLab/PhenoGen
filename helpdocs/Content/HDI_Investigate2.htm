Download marker data used to calculate eQTLs in the BXD RI panel

You can download the genetic marker data that was used to calculate the eQTLs in PhenoGen for the BXD recombinant inbred mouse strains and the HXB recombinant inbred rat strains from the Investigate QTL Regions tab. The Download marker set used in eQTL calculations link prompts you for your choice of datasets and then you click the download button to start the downloading process.
