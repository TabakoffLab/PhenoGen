You can query a pQTL interval for the genes in your gene list

You can create user-defined regions (e.g., pQTL regions; see Entering Phenotypic QTLs to set up these regions) and query them to refine the gene selection in your gene list.

  1. Access the chromosomal map view of your genes and your pQTL intervals from the Location and eQTL page of the Investigating QTL Regions tab.
  2. Choose Advanced Settings, select or define a new region of interest, then choose Physical location within region in the Configure Graphic by Region of Interest section. The gene list you download from this selection is limited to those genes in the physical location of your region of interest.
