You can use data that others have made available on this website

When you retrieve microarrays to create a new dataset, you can see that some of the arrays are labeled "Public" and others are labeled "Request Access". You can select any arrays that you want for your dataset. If you require access, the Principal Investigator the for array receives an email and can grant you access. After your access to these datasets is approved, you can finalize your dataset.
