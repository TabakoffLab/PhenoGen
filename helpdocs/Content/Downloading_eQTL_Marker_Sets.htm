Downloading eQTL Marker Sets

You can download the data files used to calculate eQTLs.

  1. Choose QTL Tools in the main menu.
  2. Click Download marker set used in eQTL calculations. The Download Marker Sets page displays.
  3. Click the checkbox next to the data file(s) you want to download.
  4. Click the Download button.
Take a look

  1. Choose to open or save the files, and follow the instructions that display. These instructions vary depending on your Internet browser (e.g., Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, etc.).
