Manually Entering a Gene List

  1. Click the Research Genes tab. The Research Genes page displays.
  2. Click Upload or create a new gene list in the What would you like to do section. The Create a New Gene List page displays.
Take a look

  1. Enter the Gene List Name.
  2. Select the Organism from the drop-down list.
  3. Enter the Gene List Description.
  4. Make sure Enter Gene List Manually is selected.
  5. Type in the genes to include in the gene list. You must separate the genes with a space or by pressing Enter on the keyboard, or they cannot be processed correctly. Genes can be entered in the following formats:

RefSeq RNA accession number

NM_010256

Begins with two capital letters, followed by an underscore, followed by numbers.

RGD ID

2203

Contains four or more numbers.

UniGene ID

Mm.4505

Contains an organism prefix (Dm, Hs, Rn, or Mm), followed by a period, followed by numbers.

Genes not entered in the formats above may not be recognized by the PhenoGen website.

  1. Click Create Gene List when you are done. Your gene list displays on the Research Genes page.
