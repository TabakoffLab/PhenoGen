Analyzing Gene Lists

Gene Analysis Tools allow you to: 

  • Analyze a gene list.See "Viewing Gene Lists" for details.
  • Upload your own data or type in a new gene list. See "Creating a Gene List Overview" for details.
  • Derive a list of genes from a microarray analysis. See "Analyzing Datasets" for details.

Using the above options, you can also delete gene lists and access tools for interpreting them.

Take a look

Gene list data security requirements depend on the origin of the gene lists. The owner of a gene list can give other users permission to see gene lists that they own. If you have permission to view a gene list, because you own it or otherwise, you can also download that gene list.
